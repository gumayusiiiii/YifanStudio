EDITORIAL DESIGN

[ PRINT DESIGN / ART PUBLICATION ]

2025

A comprehensive editorial design for a contemporary art publication, showcasing emerging artists through thoughtful typography and layout design.

ROLE

Editorial Designer, Art Director, Layout Specialist

DURATION

12 weeks

TOOLS

Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Illustrator, Acrobat

TEAM

Solo designer with editorial consultation

Overview

This editorial project involved designing a 120-page quarterly publication featuring contemporary artists and their work. The challenge was to create a layout system that could accommodate diverse artwork while maintaining typographic consistency and visual hierarchy.

The publication won the International Editorial Design Award and has been distributed to art institutions worldwide.

Design Elements

  • Custom typography system
  • Flexible grid layout
  • Color-coded sections
  • Artist interview spreads
  • Portfolio showcases
  • Special print finishes

Design Philosophy

The design approach emphasized letting the artwork speak while providing a sophisticated framework for content organization. We developed a modular grid system that could adapt to different types of content, from full-bleed artwork to detailed text passages.

Special attention was paid to paper selection and printing techniques to ensure the highest quality reproduction of the featured artworks.
