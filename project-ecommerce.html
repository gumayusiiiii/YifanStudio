E-COMMERCE

[ ONLINE STORE / ARTISAN CRAFTS ]

2025

A comprehensive e-commerce platform designed specifically for artisan craftspeople, focusing on storytelling and authentic connections between makers and buyers.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer, Frontend Developer, Product Strategy

DURATION

20 weeks

TOOLS

Figma, React, Node.js, Shopify API, Stripe

TEAM

2 Designers, 4 Developers, 1 Product Manager

Overview

This project involved designing and developing a specialized e-commerce platform that celebrates handmade crafts and the stories behind them. The challenge was creating a marketplace that felt personal and authentic while providing robust commercial functionality.

The platform launched with 150 artisan vendors and achieved $500K in sales within the first six months.

Platform Features

  • Artisan storytelling profiles
  • Process documentation tools
  • Custom product configuration
  • Direct maker communication
  • Sustainable shipping options
  • Community marketplace features

Design Philosophy

The design approach prioritized the human element of handmade goods, allowing artisans to share their processes and stories alongside their products. We developed a visual system that felt warm and authentic while maintaining the trust and functionality expected from modern e-commerce.

Special attention was paid to mobile experience, as many artisans manage their businesses primarily through mobile devices.
