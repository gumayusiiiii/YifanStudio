This project is a hand-carved miniature Panton Chair, paying tribute to Verner Panton's classic single-piece chair design.

Selected maple wood, meticulously hand-carved and polished multiple times, precisely recreating the original "S" curve and ergonomic proportions.

The surface features a high-gloss red lacquer finish, preserving the warmth of the wood while echoing a modernist style.

Verner Panton：
"Sitting should be a delight, not a duty."

Design Philosophy

Design Homage

A heartfelt tribute to Verner Panton's revolutionary single-piece chair design that forever changed furniture design thinking and manufacturing possibilities.

Precision Craftsmanship

Meticulous attention to detail using specialized micro-tools and constant reference to full-scale drawings to maintain exact proportions and ergonomic accuracy.

Material Dialogue

The bold crimson finish creates a conversation between natural wood warmth and avant-garde modernism, honoring Panton's fearless approach to color and form.

Materials & Tools

Tight-Grained Maple
Selected for fine carving and stability
Micro-Gouges
Precision carving tools for fine detail
Fine Rasps
Smoothing and shaping surfaces
Sanding Sticks
1500-3000 grit for final finish

Bold crimson finish with sealer
