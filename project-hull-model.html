Hull Model

Upcycled Wood Craft / Ship Model / Hand Carving

Category
Craftsmanship & Upcycling
Year
2021
Duration
2 Weeks
Role
Craftsman & Model Maker
Wood Carving Ship Model Upcycling Handcraft Traditional

Using the discarded wood in the woodshop, polishing and making with a carving knife, sandpaper and hand saw, a model of an ancient sailing ship was finally formed.

This project transforms waste materials into a beautiful ship model, demonstrating how traditional hand tools can breathe new life into forgotten materials.

Design Philosophy

Sustainable Upcycling

Transforming discarded wood into meaningful art, demonstrating the potential of waste materials when approached with creativity.

Traditional Craftsmanship

Using only hand tools—carving knives, sandpaper, and hand saws—to honor traditional shipbuilding methods.

Historical Inspiration

Drawing from ancient sailing ships to capture the essence and beauty of traditional maritime vessels.

Materials & Tools

Discarded Wood
Upcycled from woodshop waste
Carving Knife
Precision hand carving
Sandpaper
Smoothing and polishing
Hand Saw
Cutting and shaping

Crafting Process

01. MATERIAL COLLECTION
Selected discarded wood pieces from the woodshop, evaluating each for potential use in the ship model.
02. WOOD PREPARATION
Cleaned and prepared reclaimed wood pieces, assessing grain patterns for optimal use.
03. ROUGH SHAPING
Used hand saw to cut and shape basic forms of the ship's hull and structural elements.
04. PRECISION CARVING
Employed carving knife to create detailed features and curves of ancient sailing vessels.
05. FINAL FINISHING
Applied sandpaper for smoothing and polishing, revealing the natural beauty of the wood grain.

Technical Features

Authentic Hull Shape

Carefully crafted hull contours that accurately represent traditional shipbuilding design principles.

Natural Wood Grain

Preserved and highlighted natural grain patterns, showcasing the material's inherent beauty.

Hand-Tool Precision

Achieved fine details using only traditional hand tools, demonstrating authentic craftsmanship.
