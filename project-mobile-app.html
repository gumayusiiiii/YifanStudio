MOBILE APP

[ HEALTH & WELLNESS / USER EXPERIENCE ]

2025

A comprehensive health and wellness mobile application designed to help users track their mental and physical wellbeing through intuitive design and meaningful interactions.

ROLE

UX/UI Designer, Researcher, Prototyper

DURATION

16 weeks

TOOLS

Figma, Principle, UserTesting, Adobe Creative Suite

TEAM

2 UX Designers, 1 Researcher, 3 Developers

Overview

This project aimed to create a holistic wellness app that goes beyond simple tracking to provide meaningful insights and personalized recommendations. The challenge was designing for sensitive health data while maintaining an encouraging and non-judgmental user experience.

The app achieved a 4.8-star rating and 85% user retention rate after three months of launch.

Key Features

  • Mood and energy tracking
  • Personalized meditation sessions
  • Sleep pattern analysis
  • Social support communities
  • Progress visualization
  • Healthcare provider integration

Design Process

The design process involved extensive user research with individuals managing various wellness journeys. We conducted interviews, usability testing, and co-design sessions to ensure the app met real user needs rather than assumptions about wellness tracking.

Accessibility was a core consideration throughout, with features designed for users with different abilities and comfort levels with technology.
