Overview

This project focused on redesigning packaging for a premium organic food brand, with the goal of reducing environmental impact while enhancing shelf appeal. The challenge was balancing sustainability requirements with the premium positioning and practical needs of food packaging.

The new packaging design reduced material usage by 40% and helped the brand achieve carbon-neutral certification.

Design Elements

  • 100% recyclable materials
  • Minimal ink usage design
  • Optimized structural design
  • Clear sustainability messaging
  • Premium visual appearance
  • Enhanced product visibility

Sustainability Approach

Every design decision was evaluated through the lens of environmental impact. We conducted lifecycle assessments of different material options and printing techniques to minimize the package's carbon footprint while maintaining food safety standards.

The design features a modular system that allows for efficient manufacturing and shipping, reducing waste throughout the supply chain.
